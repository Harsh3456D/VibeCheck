Capabilities
5 Mental States. Zero Machine Learning.
🧬
Personalized Baseline
5-minute calibration learns your natural typing rhythm. Fast typists won't be falsely flagged — your normal is your zero.
⚡
Flow vs Frantic
Sustained high APM is analyzed: low error rate = you're in the zone. High error rate = you're panic-coding. Same speed, different states.
🎨
3D Shader Ribbon
Simplex noise vertex displacement with 5 reactive color palettes. Bloom post-processing for that neon glow. 120Hz+ ready.
🔒
Privacy First
Only timestamps are captured — never actual key values. All processing happens locally. Nothing is stored or transmitted.
🪟
Liquid Glass UI
Frameless, transparent, always-on-top widget with backdrop blur, dark neumorphism cards, and neon inner glows.
🦀
Rust-Powered
Tauri 2.0 backend with zero-overhead telemetry engine. Global keyboard hooks via rdev. Lightweight — no Electron bloat.
